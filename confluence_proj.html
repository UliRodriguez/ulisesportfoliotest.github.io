Team Homepage Project using Confluence

description

In this project I updated my team's homepage using Confluence and migrated their content with the rest of the organization's pages. Prior to joining my team, their homepage lived in SharePoint and was a little outdated as most of the team's resources could be accessed in Confluence.

During this process, I was paired to work with a colleague from another team that was doing a similar project task for his team. We worked together to link his team page and my team's page to the rest of the organization's pages. Along with that, we had to relink each of our team's sub-pages to correctly establish page ownership between our teams.

  • When starting this project, I use Lucid Charts to redesign my team's homepage, reorganized my team's content, and add additional information like a “Meet the Team Section”
  • I took a Confluence beginner course on LinkedIn Learning to learn how to navigate and build a page on confluence
  • Once I learned how to format text and use macros on Confluence I simply migrated my team's contents and made formatting changes

What I learned

During this project, I learned how to use Lucid Charts as a collaborating tool to design my ideas and share them with my colleague. Earned a LinkedIn certification in Learning Confluence while working on the project, helping me practice time management. Finally, I learned how to organize the content for better navigation, format bodies of text for readability, and use macros to minimize unnecessary visual noise.
